# Code of Conduct

## Keep It Simple

1. **Be respectful** - We're all here to build cool stuff
2. **Be helpful** - If someone's stuck, help them out
3. **Be patient** - Not everyone has the same experience level
4. **No harassment** - Zero tolerance for discrimination or abuse

## What We Expect

- Constructive feedback
- Respectful disagreement
- Helping newcomers
- Focusing on the code, not the person

## What We Don't Tolerate

- Personal attacks
- Harassment of any kind
- Spam or self-promotion
- Trolling

## Enforcement

Break the rules? You get warned. Keep breaking them? You get banned. Simple.

## Questions?

Open an issue or reach out on Discord.
