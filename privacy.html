Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2024

Overview

DogeGage Wallet is a non-custodial cryptocurrency wallet. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we do and don't collect.

What We DON'T Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone, address)
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Wallet addresses or balances
  • Transaction history
  • Private keys or seed phrases
  • Browsing behavior or analytics
  • Cookies for tracking

How Your Wallet Works

Your wallet is generated and encrypted locally in your browser. Your private keys never leave your device. We have no access to your funds or personal information.

Third-Party Services

Blockchain APIs: We connect to public blockchain APIs to fetch balances and broadcast transactions. These are standard blockchain operations visible to anyone.

ChangeNOW: When you use the exchange feature, you interact directly with ChangeNOW's API. Review their privacy policy separately.

Tawk.to: Our live chat support uses Tawk.to. When you use chat, Tawk.to may collect data per their privacy policy.

Local Storage

Your encrypted wallet is stored in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

Security

We use AES-256 encryption to protect your wallet. Your password never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions? Contact us via our support page.
